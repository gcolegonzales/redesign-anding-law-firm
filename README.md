# Anding Law Firm, L.L.C. — Website Redesign Concept

A single-page, fully static website concept for **Anding Law Firm, L.L.C.** (George K. Anding, Jr.), a civil-litigation practice serving **Gonzales and Ascension Parish, Louisiana**.

This is an **unsolicited redesign concept** — a "this could be yours right now" presentation piece.

## Why this concept

The firm currently has **no real website of its own**. Prospective clients vetting an attorney find only third-party directory listings — no bio, no practice-area detail, no clear way to start a conversation. For a solo civil litigator, that's a real gap: people choose lawyers they can size up online first.

This concept gives the firm a credible, professional web presence that is ready to launch:

- A confident, trustworthy visual identity (deep navy + oxblood with brass/gold accents, elegant serif headings) that signals experience and gravitas.
- A **"Request a Free Consultation"** form as the primary call to action — the firm's money feature — plus click-to-call throughout.
- Clear **practice-area cards**, an **attorney bio**, and **testimonials** so a first-time visitor immediately understands who George is and what he handles.
- Mobile-first, fast, and accessible — most people will find the firm on a phone.

## What's included

- `index.html` — the full single-page site
- `styles.css` — all styling (responsive, mobile-first, motion, reduced-motion support)
- `script.js` — sticky header, mobile nav, scroll-reveal, and the (non-wired) consultation form
- No build step, no frameworks, no npm. One Google Fonts `<link>` is the only external asset.

## How to view

Open **`index.html`** by double-clicking it, or drag it into any modern browser. Everything runs locally.
